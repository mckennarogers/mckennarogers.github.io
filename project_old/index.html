My Traveling Story

As a poor college student, I haven't been able to travel as much as I would like, but I have been able to experience some very neat places!

As a child, the main vacations my family would go on were really only to Disneyland. I absolutely adored it! We would always make a road-trip out of it and stay the night in Las Vegas.

As I grew older and entered High School, I had several opportunities to travel for competitions. Some of these places included Idaho, Arizona, California, Georgia, Virginia, and New York. I now have so many sweet memories in each of these places.

I have a goal to visit every state in the USA and have been able to mark off Washington, Hawaii, Wyoming, Montana, South Dakota, Delaware, and Maryland in addition to the states listed above.

I recently received my passport, so my travels are only expanding! The first out of country trip I was able to take was to Cape Town, South Africa. And I LOVED it!

I hope to always have a trip planned and never stop having adventures!
