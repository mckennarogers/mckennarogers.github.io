About Cape Town

Located on the Southern Coast of South Africa, Cape Town is the oldest city in South Africa and my first out-of-country experience.

  • Country: South Africa
  • When to Go: March - May and September - November
  • Supplies: Passport and Rain Jacket!
  • Best Meal: Mexican at Camps Bay Beach
  • Advice: Don't use the ATMs much, most everywhere takes card!
Cape Town Story

My Cape Town Story

I was able to experience this trip with a hand-selected group from my University in March of 2023.

We started with a 27 hour plane ride to South Africa, with layovers in Seattle, Amsterdam, and then Cape Town!

I was able to spend a week in Cape Town, so I was able to do SO MANY things! On our first day we went on a safari where we saw hippos, giraffes, lions, ostriches, wildabeasts, and zebras! We stayed at the V&A Waterfront, so we were able to explore the mall and pop up shops that were there. On our second day, we went to Mass, which was so much fun! The people there were the most welcoming people I have ever met.

Some of the other activities I was able to experience was Camps Bay Beach, the Cape of Good Hope, deep sea fishing in False Bay, Parliament, Robben Island, zip lining, PENGUINS, and volunteering at a pre-school in one of the poor townships. Volunteering at the pre-school was such a special experience. All the children there were abandoned by their parents and in the foster care system. Each was so excited to play and simply hang out with new people. They were the absolute sweetest kids.

Having my first out-of-country experience be Africa was a bit daunting and definitely jumped in head first, but I couldn't be more grateful for being able to go. I absolutely loved South Africa (expect for the 27 hours on a plane... not my favorite part...)
