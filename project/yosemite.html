About Yosemite National Park

About Yosemite National Park

Located in Eastern California, Yosemite National Park is known for its waterfalls, towering granite monolists, deep valleys, and giant sequoias.

  • State: California
  • When to Go: June - September
  • My Mode of Transportation: Car
  • Supplies: All your camping gear!
  • Best Meal: Freeze-dried pasta in the river!
  • Advice: Try to camp within Yosemite for a fun experience, but staying just outside isn't too shabby either!
Yosemite Story

My Yosemite Story

I was able to experience this trip with a friend I had just met and her friends in September of 2022.

It was just a quick weekend get-away. We drove down on a Friday and back up on a Monday. It was very tiring driving down, but definitely worth it!

The first two nights, we stayed at a campsite right outside of Yosemite. The last night, we were able to stay in the famous Camp 4. Our first day was mainly driving, but as we drove through the park, we were able to see breaktaking scenery! Our second day was filled with exploring Yosemite Valley.

The third day was my favorite though. On the third day, I was able to spend the day on my own. I hiked through the Tuolumne Meadows and along the river. After hiking, I grabbed some lunch at Yosemite Village and then mountain biked the 5-ish mile loop around the Village.

Yosemite is such a beautiful National Park! I would highly recommend going!
